Logo
& Usage

Choosing the right logo design was crucial when creating our logo as it communicates our values and our identity to our audience. A well-designed logo can create a memorable and recognizable visual identity that sets a brand apart from competitors. The Gotchu logo is a great example of how a carefully crafted design can communicate emotions and cultural nuances that resonate with our target audience.

Primary Usage

Main Brand Logo | The Gotchu logo includes 'G' and 'C,' reinforcing the brand name and identity. Its versatility allows easy adaptation to different colors and backgrounds, ensuring consistency and recognition across brand assets. The incorporation of the huggin imagery makes the Gotchu brand memorable.

'Hugo' Brand Logo | The Gotchu logo is inspired by the powerful and universal gesture of a hug. It features Hugo embracing the letter 'O,' which in Western cultures symbolizes a hug in the expression 'XO.' The closed eyes of Hugo enhance the logo's emotive aspect, conveying the warmth and comfort associated with a hug.
