Typography
& Glyphs

We chose a sans-serif typeface for Gotchu because of its modern and effortless readability. Its design is perfect for various screen sizes, and its clean lines are visually appealing. The font's calming and welcoming tone aligns with the values of our application. Poppins exemplifies a supportive and inviting tone that is necessary in setting the right atmosphere.

Description

Poppins is a modern, clean, and legible font, ideal for screen-based media. Its geometric sans-serif design maintains its legibility and integrity at different sizes, making it visually appealing and easy to read. Choosing Poppins can help create a professional and approachable impression with your written communication.
